About Me

My Pronouns: they/them

Currently Reading: Stranger in a Strange Land

Currently Watching: Twin Peaks

 I have lived in several places throughout the Pacific Northwest, but I currently (and happily) call Boise, Idaho home. I started my college journey at University of Idaho, but part way through, transferred to Boise State. And I'm glad I did, as I've fallen in love with the campus and the Boise area, and hope to spend many more years here.
 Because of my personal identity and interest in community-building, I co-founded BSU's Queer STEM Club, which aims to provide social and academic support to other LGBT+ students in STEM. Running a club post-COVID has been hard, but I'm reminded that it's worth it every time we're able to help another person.
 In my free time, I enjoy testing out new plant-based and gluten-free recipes, spending time with my feline sidekick, and working on personal projects, whether that be art, writing, or a game. I'm always down to watch a movie/show that's "so bad it's good." My most recent watch (and one I whole-heartedly recommend): Hackers(1995).
